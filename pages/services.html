My Work Approach

My consulting practice is grounded in three core principles that shape every engagement – from initial assessment to final delivery.

Participatory Methods

Stakeholders are not just subjects of evaluation or design – they are co-creators. I integrate participatory approaches throughout every phase of my work.

Gender Inclusion

Gender dynamics shape outcomes at every level. I systematically apply gender analysis to program design, evaluation, and communication to ensure no one is left behind.

Decolonial Thinking

Development work must challenge unequal power structures. I bring critical awareness to whose knowledge counts, whose voices lead, and how we measure what matters.
