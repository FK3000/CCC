Carolina Clemens
Founder & Principal Consultant

Driven by impact, guided by expertise.

With over 15 years of experience in international development and humanitarian aid, Carolina Clemens has led strategic initiatives across Africa, Asia, and Latin America. Her work focuses on creating sustainable change by strengthening local systems and building the capacities of communities.

Carolina holds advanced degrees in International Relations and Public Health, and has worked with leading international organisations including the United Nations, the World Bank, and major NGOs in complex, multi-stakeholder environments.
