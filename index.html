About Carolina Clemens

With over 15 years of experience in international development and humanitarian aid, Carolina Clemens has led strategic initiatives across Africa, Asia, and Latin America. Her expertise spans program design, capacity building, and emergency response coordination.

Carolina holds advanced degrees in International Relations and Public Health, and has worked with leading international organizations including the UN, World Bank, and major NGOs.
